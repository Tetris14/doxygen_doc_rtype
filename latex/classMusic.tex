\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a music file. 

This class is used to play background music in a game or application. The music is represented by an {\ttfamily sf\+::\+Music} object, and can be looped or played once.

Example usage\+: 
\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{\mbox{\hyperlink{classMusic}{Music}}\ music(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"{}background\_music.ogg"{}});}
\DoxyCodeLine{music.play();}
\DoxyCodeLine{...}
\DoxyCodeLine{music.stop();}

\end{DoxyCode}
